<p><p>The 3D surface topography measurement technology market comprises several key types. Optical low coherence measurement technology provides high-resolution imaging of surface structures by utilizing low coherence light sources. Confocal microscopy offers enhanced depth resolution, making it ideal for precise surface profiling. Reflection spectroscopy film thickness measurement assesses layer thickness based on light reflection variations. Digital optical microscopy combines traditional microscopy with digital imaging for detailed analysis, while 2D measurement technology focuses on surface characteristics in two dimensions, supporting various industrial applications.</p></p>
