<p><p>The PE/PET bi-component fiber market is divided into staple fiber and filament segments. Conversely, filament fibers are continuous strands used in applications like elastics and high-performance textiles, offering strength and durability. Both types leverage the unique properties of polyethylene (PE) and polyethylene terephthalate (PET) to enhance performance, contributing to a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
