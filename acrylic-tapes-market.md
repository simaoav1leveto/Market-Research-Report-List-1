<p><p>The acrylic tapes market is categorized mainly into double-sided and single-sided tapes. Double-sided acrylic tapes feature adhesive on both sides, making them ideal for mounting and bonding applications where both surfaces require adhesion. Single-sided acrylic tapes, on the other hand, have adhesive on one side and are commonly used for protection, insulation, and surface mounting. Both types provide excellent durability, weather resistance, and adhesion strength, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
