<p><p>Microporous insulation panels are high-performance thermal insulators, classified into two main types: flexible and rigid. Flexible microporous insulation panels are lightweight and offer excellent thermal resistance, making them ideal for applications where ease of installation and flexibility are required. In contrast, rigid microporous insulation panels provide enhanced structural support and durability, making them suitable for high-temperature and demanding environments. Both types are used in industries such as construction, aerospace, and refrigeration, contributing to energy efficiency and thermal management.</p></p>
