<p><p>The fasciotomy devices market includes various types designed for effective wound management and healing. Split-thickness skin grafts (STSG) involve transplanting a slice of skin to cover wounds, while negative pressure wound therapy (NPWT) uses suction to promote healing by drawing out fluids. Vessel loop techniques help manage blood flow in compromised tissues, and continuous external tissue expansion (CETE) gradually stretches skin for grafting. Other market options may include innovative wound dressings and advanced surgical tools tailored for specific patient needs.</p></p>
