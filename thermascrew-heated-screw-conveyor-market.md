<p><p>The Thermascrew Heated Screw Conveyor market comprises two primary heating types: electric heating and media thermal regulation. Electric heating utilizes resistive elements to directly heat the conveyor, ensuring precise temperature control and efficient material processing. In contrast, media thermal regulation involves using thermal fluids or gases to transfer heat indirectly, maintaining a consistent temperature throughout the material. Both methods enhance the handling of viscous or temperature-sensitive materials, improving operational efficiency and product quality in various industrial applications.</p></p>
