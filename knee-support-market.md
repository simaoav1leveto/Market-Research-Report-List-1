<p><p>The knee support market can be categorized into two main types: rigid and soft supports. Rigid knee supports are designed to provide maximum stability and support, often used post-injury or for conditions requiring immobilization. They typically feature hard materials and braces that limit movement. In contrast, soft knee supports offer flexibility and gentle compression, making them suitable for mild sprains, strains, or preventative use during physical activities. Both types serve distinct purposes based on the level of support needed.</p></p>
