<p><strong>The Pillow Plate Heat Exchangers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Embossed</li><li>Double Embossed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pillow plate heat exchangers are categorized into single embossed and double embossed types, each serving distinct thermal management needs. Single embossed pillow plates feature a single layer of embossed surfaces, ideal for applications with lower pressure and heat transfer requirements. In contrast, double embossed pillow plates consist of two embossed layers, providing enhanced strength and improved thermal efficiency, making them suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Both types are widely used across industries, including food processing, chemical, and pharmaceutical sectors.</p></p>
