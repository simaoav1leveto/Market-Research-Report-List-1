<p><p>The spectacle lens market primarily encompasses two main types: single vision lenses and progressive lenses. Single vision lenses cater to individuals needing correction for one field of vision, either distance or near. This innovative design enhances visual comfort and convenience for those with presbyopia, making both lens types essential in addressing diverse vision correction needs across various demographics.</p></p>
